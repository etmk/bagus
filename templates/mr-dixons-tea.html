Mr Dixon's Tea

An informal guide to creating the perfect brew.

Prepare the mug

Mr Dixon's mug is not any old mug. It should be clean to ensure the beverage is not tainted.

Prepare the tea bag

Be sure to verify that the tea bag is not scrunched or crushed. Mr Dixon's tea will not be perfect unless the tea has room to breathe.

Steep

Pour boiled water into the mug leaving 1.5cm (15mm or 0.6") clearance. Stir once, do not play with it.

The tea bag should only be removed from the water after 2 minutes. Squeeze the tea bag on it's departure to ensure Mr Dixon receives all the nutrients and a full flavour.

Milk

Under no circumstances should milk be added before the steeping process has been completed. This is a crime, punishable by death.

Add semi-skimmed milk slowly while stirring the beverage. Mr Dixon will not accept a milky tea.

Please review the colour with the guide below.

  • HEX: #BF9474
  • CYMK: 8, 41, 51, 20
  • RGB: 191, 148, 116
  • HSL: 26, 37, 60

Announce the beverage

Mr Dixon is a busy man. On arriving to Mr Dixon's desk, be sure to announce the tea's arrival with a short "Ta da!". Without proper announcement, the arrival will be missed and the tea will go cold.
